What We Do

Housing that puts people first

We offer a range of housing options and support services designed around the needs of our residents — from affordable rented homes to shared ownership and community wellbeing programmes.

Affordable Rented Homes

Quality homes at below-market rents across England and Wales, helping families and individuals find a secure, stable place to live.

Shared Ownership

A flexible route onto the property ladder — purchase a share of your home and pay rent on the rest, with options to buy more over time.

Our Services

Everything you need under one roof

As a registered provider of social housing, Havenmark Homes offers a comprehensive range of housing and support services to current and prospective residents.

1

Affordable Rented Homes

Below-market rents charged at no more than 80% of local market rates, regulated by Homes England and the Regulator of Social Housing.

2

Shared Ownership Schemes

Buy between 10%–75% of a property and pay reduced rent on the remainder, with the right to staircase up to 100% ownership.

3

Sheltered & Supported Housing

Specialist accommodation for older residents and those with additional needs, with on-site support and communal facilities.

4

Temporary & Emergency Housing

Working with local councils to provide temporary accommodation for households in urgent housing need across our areas of operation.

Regulated & Accredited

Havenmark Homes is registered with the Regulator of Social Housing and is a member of the National Housing Federation. We comply with the Consumer Standards and publish our performance data annually.

Repairs & Maintenance

Report a repair or maintenance issue

We aim to complete all routine repairs within 28 days and urgent repairs within 24 hours. Emergency repairs are attended to within 4 hours.

Emergency? If you have a gas leak, flooding, or total loss of heating in winter, call our emergency line immediately: 0300 123 4567 — available 24/7.

Emergency Repairs

Gas leaks, flooding, no heating in winter, structural damage — attended within 4 hours.

Urgent Repairs

Part loss of heating, leaking roof, broken door locks, unsafe electrics — completed within 24 hours.

Routine Repairs

General maintenance, plastering, minor plumbing — completed within 28 calendar days.
